About Us

From Kochi's spice docks to the Mother City.

Arjun Menon arrived in Cape Town in 1994 with a trunk of cardamom, pepper, and handwritten family recipes from Kerala's coast. He first traded spices to neighbourhood cooks and hotel kitchens, then opened the restaurant those conversations kept asking for.

Today his kitchen folds Indian spice-craft into South African ingredients: kingklip from cold Atlantic waters, Karoo lamb, Cape Malay aromatics, and bright peninsula produce.
