About Us

From Kochi's spice docks to the Mother City.

Arjun Menon arrived in Cape Town in 1994 with a trunk of cardamom, pepper, and handwritten family recipes from Kerala's coast. At first, he traded spices to neighbourhood cooks, hotel kitchens, and curious chefs who wanted heat with history.

The Spice Peninsula is the restaurant that grew from those conversations. Arjun's kitchen folds Indian spice-craft into South African ingredients: kingklip from cold Atlantic waters, Karoo lamb, Cape Malay aromatics, and the bright produce of the peninsula. The result is food that feels both travelled and rooted.
